Visiting Nursing – Reliable Health Support at Home

MSC Elderly Care offers expert visiting nursing services throughout Osaka and Hyogo, bringing professional care directly to the homes of those in need. Our trained nurses assist with daily health monitoring, medical treatments, rehabilitation, and emotional support, all under the guidance of physicians. From help with bathing and medication to advanced medical equipment use and end-of-life care, our goal is to support comfort, safety, and independence at home. With a dedicated coordination team and a responsive care system, we ensure fast, compassionate service tailored to each individual.

At MSC Elderly Care, our Visiting Nursing service provides skilled, compassionate healthcare for seniors in the comfort of their own homes. Serving Osaka and Hyogo, our licensed nurses work closely with doctors and families to deliver medical support tailored to each individual’s needs. Services include daily health checks, medication guidance, in-home treatments, rehabilitation, and support with medical equipment. We also provide emotional care and family consultations, ensuring peace of mind for everyone involved.

What we provide

Daily Life Assistance

  • Bathing, hair washing, oral care, toileting, meal assistance, and mobility support.

Medical Management

  • Administering medications and injections
  • Monitoring vital signs (temperature, pulse, blood pressure)
  • Assisting with IVs, oxygen therapy, catheter care, and suctioning

Rehabilitation & Recovery

  • Swallowing therapy and mobility exercises
  • Support to prevent decline in physical and cognitive function
  • Pain relief, pressure ulcer prevention, and wound care

Mental & Emotional Support

  • Dementia care and daily routine guidance
  • Emotional counseling for patients and families
  • End-of-life care and terminal support

Equipment Support

  • Setup and training for oxygen machines, feeding tubes, ventilators, and more

Our care homes offer 24/7 support, meals, activities, and medical monitoring in a warm, community-focused environment.

Trained caregivers assist with daily tasks, health checks, and companionship, helping seniors live independently in their own homes.
