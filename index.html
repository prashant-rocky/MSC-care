Questions & answers

Frequently asked questions

  • How do I know if my parent received their medication reminder?
    You’ll get a notification or email update once the reminder is delivered and acknowledged by your parent, so you’re always informed.
  • Can I choose how the reminders and updates are delivered?
    Yes! We offer phone calls, app notifications, or a combination of both — depending on what best suits your loved one’s needs.
  • What happens if there’s no response during a check-in?
    If we can’t reach your loved one after multiple attempts, we will immediately notify the registered family contact to ensure their safety.
  • Is my family’s personal information safe and secure?
    Absolutely. We follow strict data protection policies and use secure systems to ensure your loved one’s personal and health information is fully protected.
