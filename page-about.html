About Our Company

Six businesses specializing in medical and welfare services

MSC started in 2015 as a consulting business for the operation of visiting nursing stations. Currently, in addition to this visiting nursing business, we operate six other business formats, including elderly care facility management, visiting care, insurance pharmacies, home care support, and housing referrals. By specializing in medical and welfare services, we are providing a safe, secure, and comfortable lifestyle in an aging society.
